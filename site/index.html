The Helmfolio difference

Measured the way traders think — in cycles

A cycle is one complete round trip in a symbol: from the moment you open a position until you close it flat again. Scale in, add, trim, scale out — it's still one cycle, one decision.

Most tools score every fill or every day on its own — which inflates your trade count and distorts your win rate. Helmfolio groups every fill into the trade you actually made, so your statistics judge decisions, not noise.

Cycle win rate

The share of completed cycles that closed in profit — how often your trade ideas actually work.

Cycle payoff ratio

Your average winning cycle divided by your average losing cycle — are your winners bigger than your losers?

Cycle profit factor

Gross profit from your winning cycles over gross loss from the losers — the single number that says if your edge is real.

Connected in about 3 minutes

Two keys from your IBKR Client Portal. Paste once. Sync forever.

1

Generate your Flex Token

In IBKR Client Portal, go to Performance & Reports → Flex Queries and generate a Flex Web Service token.

2

Create an Activity Flex Query

Include Trades, Open Positions, Cash Transactions and NAV; set format to XML, period Last 365 days. Copy the Query ID.

3

Paste & sync

Drop both into Helmfolio's Settings (encrypted on your machine) and click Sync IBKR Flex Now. Done.

A full step-by-step setup guide is included inside the app.

Frequently asked questions

Does anything get uploaded to the cloud?

Everything stays on your own computer. Your trades, positions, NAV history, cash transactions and settings are saved only on your machine — never on our servers and never in the cloud.

Is my IBKR Flex Token safe?

Yes. Your Flex Token and Query ID are stored on your own computer, encrypted by your operating system. They're never written in plaintext and never uploaded anywhere. When you sync, your computer talks directly to IBKR's official Flex Web Service over HTTPS.

Do you support brokers other than IBKR?

No. Helmfolio is engineered exclusively for Interactive Brokers using their Flex Query system. We'd rather do one broker exceptionally well than many superficially.

How much history can I analyze?

Helmfolio is built around IBKR's 365-day Flex window and merges every sync into a growing local history — duplicate trades are skipped automatically — so your record keeps building over time on your own machine.

Can I use my license on more than one computer?

A single Pro license can be activated on 2 of your personal devices. Moving to a new machine? Deactivate the old device inside the app first to free a seat, then activate the new one.

How do I back up my data or move to a new computer?

Yes. In Settings you can export your entire dataset to a single JSON file — every trade, position, NAV snapshot and cash transaction — and import it back on any machine to pick up exactly where you left off. You can also open your local data folder straight from Settings to copy or archive it yourself. Because everything lives on your computer, your records are yours to back up and carry anywhere.

Who is behind Helmfolio?

Helmfolio is built by an independent developer who trades on IBKR — designed by a trader, for traders. For anything you need, reach us at support@helmfolio.com.
